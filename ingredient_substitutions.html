Ingredient Substitutions


Here are some common dairy substitutions:

  • Milk: Use almond milk, coconut milk, soy milk, or oat milk as a replacement. I use coconut milk for baking
  • Butter: Substitute with coconut oil, olive oil, or margarine. I use Imperial brand margarine for baking
  • Yogurt: Try soy yogurt, coconut yogurt. I use So Delicious unsweeetened yogurt in baking or sauces.
  • Cheese: You can use nutritional yeast, cashew cheese, or vegan cheese alternatives.
    I am not personally a fan of dairy free cheese. Vegan Cream Cheese is made pretty well
  • Heavy Cream: Replace with coconut cream or cashew cream.
